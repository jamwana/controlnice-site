About ControlNice

Precision Measurement, Engineered for the World.

Who We Are

ControlNice Measurement Technology (Tianjin) Co., Ltd. is a precision metrology equipment manufacturer headquartered in Tianjin, China.

Founded with a clear mission — to make high-precision, large-scale measurement accessible to manufacturers worldwide — ControlNice specializes in Articulated Arm Coordinate Measuring Machines (AACMM) and Portable CMM Solutions.

From our modern manufacturing facility in Shuanggang Industrial Park, Jinnan District, we design, produce, and ship metrology equipment to customers across the globe. Every unit is calibrated and tested to international standards before leaving our factory.

What We Build

ControlNice manufactures a complete range of Articulated Arm CMMs and Portable Metrology Solutions:

  • AACMM Series: High-precision articulated arm measuring machines with ranges from 1.2m to 4.5m
  • Portable CMM Systems: Complete measurement kits with probes, scanners, and software
  • Accessories: Touch probes, laser scanners, magnetic bases, tripods, and calibration artifacts

All products come with a 3-year warranty — a testament to our confidence in build quality.
