CNA-P/E Series — Portable Articulated Arm CMM

The CNA-E and CNA-P are two portable articulated arms CMM, ideal for quick and accurate inspection of any parts within its range. The result of nearly 30 years of experience in production of portable measurement arms. Now with better accuracy, repeatability, and a new design.

  • CNA-P: 1.8m to 9.0m diameter range, 6 or 7 axis configuration
  • CNA-E: 1.8m to 4.0m diameter range, 6 axis only (entry-level)
  • Improved accuracy and repeatability with new design
  • Lighter arms for easier handling during measuring
  • Double spring counter-balance system
  • Magnetic brake to prevent accidental fall (CNA-P)
  • Compatible with laser scanners, touch probes, and laser forks
  • Interfaced with many 3rd party inspection or reverse-engineering software

High Precision On-line Measuring Arm

Portable CMM that requires no fixed installation — designed for high-precision measurement on the shop floor. Industry-leading accuracy of 8 microns (ISO 10360-2).

  • Max accuracy: 8 microns (ISO 10360-2)
  • Measurement range: 1.3 m to 1.8 m
  • Innovative balance system — effortless handling
  • "Home Position" safety feature — no locks needed
  • Range: 1.3 m to 1.8 m measurement diameter
  • Accuracy: 0.019 mm to 0.2 mm depending on length

CNA-T

Laser Fork for Tube Measurement

Non-contact laser fork system for inspecting tubes, hoses, and pipes. Directly interfaces with CNC tube benders for adjustment and springback correction.

  • Non-contact laser scanning of tubes, hoses, and pipes
  • Laser fork sizes: 30 mm, 50 mm, 80 mm, 150 mm, 200 mm
  • Tube diameter range: 1 mm to 180 mm
  • Direct interface with CNC tube benders for adjustment & springback correction
  • Larger diameters available with hard probe

HX Series

Huaxia Arm — High Precision Joint Arm CMM

HX series joint arm CMM measures dimensions, shape, and position accuracy of complex parts. Features flexible use, accurate and efficient measurement, and easy portability.

  • Infinite rotation of the main axis — no measurement dead zone
  • Safety protection lock — prevents accidental drops
  • Built-in temperature compensation system (0–45 °C)
  • Installation: Circular magnetic chuck, vacuum chuck, or threaded connection
  • Built-in lithium battery — up to 8 hours of operation
  • Connectivity: USB (wired) or Bluetooth (wireless)
  • HX6-E: Standard series for general precision
  • HX6-Non-standard: Micron-level accuracy for mold processing, aerospace, research
  • Range: 2.0 m to 4.5 m
