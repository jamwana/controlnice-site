AACMM — The Flagship Precision Measuring Arm

ControlNice's Articulated Arm CMM combines large-scale reach with micron-level repeatability. Designed for demanding industrial environments, the AACMM series delivers lab-grade accuracy directly on the shop floor.

  • Measurement Range: 1.2m to 4.5m working envelope
  • Repeatability: ≤ 0.015mm (15μm) across full range
  • High-resolution absolute encoders on all axes
  • Touch trigger, scanning, and laser scanner compatible
  • Tripod, magnetic base, or table-mount configurations
  • Compatible with leading metrology software packages
  • ISO 10360 compliant, traceable calibration certificate included

Portable CMM — Metrology Without Boundaries

Take precision measurement to the part, not the part to the lab. ControlNice Portable CMM solutions enable accurate 3D inspection anywhere — on the production line, in the assembly bay, or at the supplier's facility.

  • Complete System: Arm, controller, probe set, software, rugged case
  • Quick Setup: Deploy in under 10 minutes, no fixed installation
  • Bluetooth/WiFi data transmission for untethered operation
  • Touch probe, laser scanning, and photogrammetry integration
