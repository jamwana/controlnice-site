Aerospace & Defense

Large-Scale Precision for Aerospace Components

The Challenge

Aircraft structures, engine housings, and wing assemblies demand micron-level accuracy across multi-meter dimensions. Traditional CMMs are limited by their fixed gantry size.

ControlNice Solution

  • Portable AACMM reaches up to 9m, measuring entire fuselage sections on-site
  • Non-destructive inspection with touch probe and laser scanning
  • Compatible with aerospace quality standards (AS9100, NADCAP)
  • Real-time GD&T analysis for first-article inspection

Key Applications

Wing spar and rib measurement
Engine nacelle and casing verification
Landing gear component inspection
Composite panel dimensional control

Shipbuilding & Marine Engineering

Measuring the World's Largest Structures

The Challenge

Ship hulls, propellers, and marine engines are among the largest and most complex industrial components. Transporting them to a measurement lab is impossible.

ControlNice Solution

  • Long-range AACMM (up to 9m) for on-site hull section measurement
  • Magnetic base mounting directly onto ferrous ship structures
  • Dust and splash resistant for dockyard environments
  • Reverse engineering capability for legacy vessel repair

Key Applications

Hull block assembly verification
Propeller blade profile measurement
Marine engine cylinder and crankshaft inspection
Pipe routing and flange alignment

New Energy & Wind Power

Precision for the Energy Transition

The Challenge

Wind turbine blades, tower sections, and gearbox housings require high-accuracy measurement of very large, complex curved surfaces — often in outdoor or semi-outdoor environments.

ControlNice Solution

  • Large-envelop AACMM covers entire blade sections from a single setup
  • Portable system deployable at manufacturing sites and wind farms
  • Temperature-compensated measurement for outdoor use
  • Laser scanning for full-surface blade profiling

Key Applications

Wind turbine blade quality control
Tower flange and bolt hole pattern inspection
Gearbox housing dimensional verification
Solar panel frame and mounting structure inspection
